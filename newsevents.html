September 18, 2017:

Civil Engineering’s faculty Dr. Zhou is approved by SIU Trustees to serve on ERTC Advisory Committee

Civil Engineering department's Professor Jianpeng (Jim) Zhou has been approved by SIU Trustees to serve on the ERTC Advisory Committee. Please see the full news release.

August 29, 2017:

Akash Jain, is the recipient of the 2017 ASCE/SEI St. Louis Chapter Structural Engineering Scholarship

SIUE CE graduate student, Akash Jain, is the recipient of the 2017 ASCE/SEI St. Louis Chapter Structural Engineering Scholarship. He will receive this scholarship at the St. Louis Section annual dinner. This structural scholarship has been offered since 2013 by the St. Louis Chapter of ASCE/SEI to a student pursuing a Master’s degree with a structural emphasis enrolled during the Fall semester at a Midwest University.

April 26, 2017:

CE Students Build Masonry Arch

On April 26, Mark Vasquez of MAV Masonry helped a team of civil engineering students construct a masonry arch in our structural laboratory. Building Products Corporation and MAV graciously donated 200 bricks, lime, and sand for the project, which is part of a masonry arch competition sponsored by the Association for Preservation Technology International.
